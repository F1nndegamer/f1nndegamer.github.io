Privacy Policy for Magnet Madness

Last updated: May 5, 2025

1. Who We Are

Magnet Madness is developed by an independent team. You can contact us at f1nndevgamer@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following data:

  • Username – chosen by the player
  • Personal Best (PB) Time – your best time in the game

This data is collected automatically and is not linked to any external accounts.

3. Why We Collect This Data

The data is used solely for the purpose of maintaining an in-game leaderboard.

4. How We Share Your Data

Your username and PB time are shared with a third-party leaderboard service:

Dan's Leaderboard Creator – used to display in-game rankings.

No other third parties receive your data.

5. User Rights

You may request deletion of your data by emailing f1nndevgamer@gmail.com with your in-game username.

6. Data Location

This game is primarily used in the Netherlands, and we aim to comply with GDPR. The data collected does not directly identify users.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. Parents can contact us for data removal if needed.

8. Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your data (username and personal best time), please send an email to f1nndevgamer@gmail.com with your in-game username.

We will delete your data from our systems, including our leaderboard, within 14 days of receiving your request.

What will be deleted: Your username and personal best time.

What will be retained: No personally identifiable data is stored. Once deleted, your information cannot be recovered.
